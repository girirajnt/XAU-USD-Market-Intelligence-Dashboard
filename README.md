# XAU-USD-Market-Intelligence-Dashboard
A professional Power BI Dashboard for analysing historical XAU/USD market data through candlestick pattern analysis, market trend, volatility insights, and interactive business intelligence visualisation. 


## Overview

The XAU/USD Analytics Dashboard is a professional Power BI project developed to analyze historical Gold (XAU/USD) candlestick data. This dashboard transforms raw OHLC market data into meaningful business insights through interactive visualizations, advanced DAX calculations, and dynamic filtering.

The project provides a comprehensive analysis of market direction, candlestick patterns, volatility, trading performance, and price behavior across multiple time dimensions including hour, day, month, and quarter. It is designed to help traders and analysts make data-driven decisions by identifying market trends and high-probability trading opportunities.


## Project Objectives

- Analyze historical XAU/USD market data.
- Compare bullish and bearish market movements.
- Monitor candlestick pattern frequency.
- Measure market volatility across different time periods.
- Identify the best trading hours.
- Evaluate candlestick pattern performance.
- Build an interactive and professional Power BI dashboard.
